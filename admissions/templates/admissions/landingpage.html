About FG College

Situated in the heart of Peshawar Cantonment, along the school road

FG Degree College for Men Peshawar came into existence in the year 1965 as a result of up-graduation of Cantt Public School to the status of an intermediate college. The college was upgraded to Degree level in 1979. The college has replaced 2-year BA/B.SC Programs with 4-years BS programs in two disciplines. i.e BS Computer and BS Political Science

It is affiliated with Peshawar as well as Federal Board at inter level and University of Peshawar has granted affiliation for degree classes
